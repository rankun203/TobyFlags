Toby Flags

"Toby Flags" is a hostel that hosts people of color from all around the world. Not only can you meet people of different races, you can share and exchange culture experiences, share your stories with each other, and enjoy the great atmosphere at all times. Toby Flags organizes activities such as: International parties, Spanish corners, bible clubs, art exhibitions, micro films, concerts, skateboard parties, etc. Welcome you guys to join us.

Activity

Toby Flags organizes free and inexpensive tours, activities and events for guests. We organizes activities such as: International parties, Spanish corners, bible clubs, art exhibitions, micro films, concerts, skateboard parties, etc. Check out the activity boards for an updated schedule of events during your stay. If you would prefer to venture out on your own, Toby Flags can help guide you.

Hostel

Toby Flags got some room for visitors, feature and quiet, makes you feel like warm home. There is very comfortable dinning room, living room, terrace and a spiral stair. We spend a lot of time on provide a better feeling for both travellers and lodger.
